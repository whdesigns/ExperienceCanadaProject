What is Canada?

Canada is a country which offers a little bit of everything. The people are regarded as friendly and courteous. The wildlife has not yet been destroyed by poachers or the metropolitan area. Some compare Canada with America, and in some ways the two countries are quite similar, but Canada is more naturalistic and if you visit places like Lake Louise or Niagara Falls you will see why.
